Our Story

About BlinkTools

BlinkTools is a simple collection of free online utilities designed to be fast, mobile-friendly, and easy to use — without any of the usual friction.

Our goal is to build tools people actually need daily — without annoying popups, forced sign-ups, or heavy pages that take forever to load.

Why we built this

Most online tools are bloated, ad-ridden, and require you to create an account just to count words or generate a QR code. That's unnecessary. Every tool on BlinkTools runs entirely in your browser — your data never touches our servers because we don't have any server processing at all.

What we offer

Currently we have four core tools: a Word / Character Counter, a QR Code Generator, an Image Compressor, and a Password Generator. All free, all instant, all private.

Privacy first

We take privacy seriously. No tracking pixels, no analytics that identify you, no selling data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Get in touch

Have a tool suggestion or found a bug? Contact us — we'd love to hear from you.
