Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

BlinkTools ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect (spoiler: very little) and how we handle it.

Data Collection

All BlinkTools utilities (word counter, QR generator, image compressor, password generator) process data entirely inside your browser. Text you type, images you upload, and passwords you generate are never sent to any server. We cannot see them.

Analytics

We may use basic, privacy-respecting analytics (such as page view counts) that do not identify individual users and do not use cookies that track you across sites.

Advertising

We plan to use Google AdSense to display ads once approved. Google may use cookies to show relevant ads. You can opt out via Google's Ad Settings. We do not control the content of third-party ads.

Cookies

BlinkTools itself does not set cookies. Any cookies present are set by third-party services (e.g. Google AdSense) and are governed by their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

BlinkTools is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new date.
