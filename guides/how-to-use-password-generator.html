How to Use a Password Generator Safely

Using strong passwords is one of the most important steps to protect your online accounts. A password generator helps you create random, hard-to-guess passwords in seconds.

Why strong passwords matter

Weak or reused passwords can be easily guessed or cracked. Strong passwords reduce the risk of hacking and data theft.

How to use a password generator

  1. Choose a password length of at least 12 characters
  2. Enable uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  3. Generate a password and copy it
  4. Store it securely in a password manager

Is it safe?

BlinkTools processes everything in your browser. Your generated passwords are never sent to a server.
